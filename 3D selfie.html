What is a 3D Selfie?

Simply put, it's a miniature statue, 3D printed of course!

Instead of flat, 2D pictures, easily get a 3D printed version of yourself, your family, and friends! Aakist' Selfies make for fun desk toys, great holiday gifts, unique display pieces, or anything you can think of! They're a great way to capture special moments in life in a new, unique way.

How is 3D scanning done?

If you live in the India, you can visit a location that offers scanning. Strike a pose and hold it for 2 minutes. The scanner will move around you, capturing your image as a 3D digital file. The file will be automatically uploaded to Aakist, and you'll get an email with a link to purchase. You can select from a variety of materials, including Full Color Sandstone, which captures color and details really well. You can also get a 3D Selfie in Steel, Brass, or even Silver and Gold!
